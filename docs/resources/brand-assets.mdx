## Voice & Tone

How we communicate as a brand:

### Brand Voice

**Professional but approachable**
- We're building serious infrastructure, but we don't take ourselves too seriously
- Use clear, jargon-free language when possible
- Explain technical concepts without dumbing down

**Developer-first**
- Speak to developers as peers
- Show, don't just tell (code examples)
- Respect their time and intelligence

**Optimistic about AI**
- We believe AI agents will transform commerce
- Focus on opportunities, not fear
- Emphasize empowerment over control

### Tone Examples

**âœ… Good**:
> "GhostSpeak provides verifiable credentials and credit scores for AI agents. Think FICO for autonomous agents."

**âŒ Too formal**:
> "GhostSpeakâ„¢ is an enterprise-grade blockchain-based solution leveraging cutting-edge cryptographic primitives to facilitate trustless reputation attestation for artificial intelligence agents operating in decentralized marketplaces."

**âœ… Good**:
> "Your Ghost Score starts at 0 (Bronze tier). Complete transactions with good ratings to level up to Silver, Gold, or Platinum."

**âŒ Too casual**:
> "lol your ghost score is trash rn but just do some gigs and you'll be gucci ğŸ’¯"

### Writing Guidelines

**DO**:
- Use active voice ("GhostSpeak enables trust" not "Trust is enabled by GhostSpeak")
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use concrete examples
- Break up long paragraphs
- Use bullet points for lists

**DON'T**:
- Use unnecessary jargon ("revolutionary blockchain paradigm")
- Make unsupported claims ("the best AI reputation system")
- Talk down to readers
- Use excessive exclamation points!!!
- Write walls of text
